Dt 2.          Paragraph settings (Indent and Spacing)

A paragraph (or anything at all) consists of two main spaces:-

1.       Indent (Space from left, and from right), and

2.       Spacing (Size of gap between the lines and between the paragraph)
The difference between lines and paragraph are:

a.       Paragraph spacing: The space that is applied when you press ENTER, which indicates a new paragraph, and

b.       Line spacing: The space between the same paragraph (OR pressing SHIFT+ENTER, which force line break without entering to the next paragraph.)

Personalization Section

There are various customization options available which could improve your user experience by reducing the number of clicks on the tabs.

Pz 1.          Office Themes

By accessing File > Options (bottom-left) > Personalize your copy of Microsoft Office.

There are various choices of office theme, for those who are fan of dark mode can select "Black" and either tick or untick "Never change the document page color" for long editing sessions.

Pz 2.          Ribbon Edit

The "ribbons" are the tabs that is on the top of the MS Office, you can create a custom ribbon, or hide several unwanted features of the default ribbon.

Right click any of the tabs, then select Customize the Ribbon
